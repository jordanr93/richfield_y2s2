Spotting Trends and Patterns

Data visualization makes it easier to detect trends and patterns that may be hard to see in raw
numbers. For example, a time series graph might reveal sales fluctuations over the year, helping
identify seasonal patterns. Visualizations like scatter plots or histograms can also show outliers
or unusual data points that may require strategic adjustments.
