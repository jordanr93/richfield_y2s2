3.3 IoT Standards

The adoption of IoT standards is critical to ensuring seamless interoperability, security, and
efficiency within the diverse ecosystem of connected devices and applications. These standards
define protocols and frameworks that facilitate communication and integration, enabling devices
from different manufacturers and operating in varied environments to work together
harmoniously. Below are key categories of IoT standards, including those related to connectivity,
security, and privacy.
