   Descriptive Analytics

   Descriptive analytics focuses on summarizing and interpreting historical data to understand
   what has happened. It looks at past performance and gives you insights into trends, patterns,
   and behaviors. For example, a company might use descriptive analytics to analyze last year's
   sales data and see which products performed best, or how customer behavior changed over
   time. Essentially, it’s about answering the question: What happened?
