2.6.3 Industrial IoT (IIoT)

The Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) connects machines, sensors, and systems within industries
to enhance productivity, improve safety, and streamline operations. IIoT applications support the
digital transformation of industries, enabling smarter manufacturing processes.

   Applications:

           Predictive Maintenance: By analyzing sensor data, IIoT systems can predict equipment
           failures before they happen, reducing downtime and costly repairs.

           Supply Chain Optimization: Real-time tracking of goods through IoT enables more
           efficient inventory and logistics management, improving the speed and accuracy of
           deliveries.

           Smart Factories: IoT enables factories to automate processes, monitor machinery, and
           improve quality control, ensuring more efficient production lines.

   Benefits:

           IIoT contributes to reduced operational costs, improved productivity, and enhanced
           safety for workers.
