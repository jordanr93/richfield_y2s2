Security and Data Breaches

Ethical and legal obligations around data security are critical in big data strategy. Organizations
must ensure that they take appropriate measures to protect sensitive information from
unauthorized access or breaches. This includes using encryption, secure storage, and regular
audits. A failure to protect data could result in significant harm to individuals, particularly if the
data includes sensitive personal information like credit card details or health data.

Legally, businesses are required to notify individuals in case of a data breach and must follow
specific protocols to mitigate damage. Depending on the jurisdiction, failing to comply with
security standards can result in hefty fines and lawsuits.
