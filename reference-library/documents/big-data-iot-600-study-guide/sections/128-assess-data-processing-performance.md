Assess Data Processing Performance

Regularly evaluate how fast your data is being processed and whether there are bottlenecks. If
data isn’t moving quickly enough or your systems are slowing down, it’s time to adjust. Scaling
your infrastructure or improving your data pipelines may be necessary, especially as the amount
of data grows. Monitoring throughput (how much data you can process) and latency (how fast
data is processed) will help you identify any weak points.
