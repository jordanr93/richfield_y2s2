2.6.8 Retail

IoT technology is reshaping the retail sector by enabling personalized customer experiences,
smarter inventory management, and seamless shopping experiences.

   Applications:

          Smart Shelves: These shelves detect when stock levels are low and notify staff to
          replenish items, ensuring efficient inventory management.

          Beacon Technology: By using Bluetooth, IoT beacons send personalized offers to
          customers' smartphones based on their location within the store, enhancing the
          shopping experience.

          IoT-enabled Checkout Systems: Stores like Amazon Go use IoT to eliminate the need
          for traditional checkout counters, offering customers a faster and more efficient
          shopping experience.

   Benefits:

          Retailers benefit from improved customer satisfaction, streamlined operations, and
          better inventory management.
