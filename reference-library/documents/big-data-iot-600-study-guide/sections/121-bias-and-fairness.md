Bias and Fairness

Big data algorithms are often used to make decisions that affect individuals, such as loan
approvals, hiring processes, or insurance pricing. However, there is a risk that these algorithms
could perpetuate bias if they are trained on biased data or designed without considering fairness.
For example, an algorithm trained on biased historical data might disproportionately
disadvantage certain groups, leading to discrimination.
