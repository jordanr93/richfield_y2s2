Tracking Key Metrics

Dashboards are commonly used for visualizing KPIs. They provide a quick, at-a-glance view of
important metrics such as sales performance, customer satisfaction, or operational efficiency.
Visualizing these metrics allows stakeholders to track how well the strategy is performing in real-
time. For example, a dashboard might show:

   •   Sales growth (line chart).
   •   Customer retention (gauge or bar chart).
   •   Marketing ROI (scatter plot or bubble chart).

This gives teams a clear view of performance across different areas of the business.
