2.6.9 Environmental Monitoring

IoT technologies are essential for monitoring and managing environmental conditions, aiding in
efforts to combat pollution, climate change, and wildlife conservation.

   •   Applications:

          Air Quality Sensors: These devices monitor pollution levels in urban areas, helping
          cities manage air quality and protect public health.

          Water Quality Systems: IoT-enabled systems detect contamination in water sources,
          providing real-time alerts to mitigate health risks.

          Wildlife Tracking Systems: IoT devices monitor the movements and behaviors of
          endangered species, aiding conservation efforts and habitat protection.

   Benefits:

          IoT applications in environmental monitoring contribute to better conservation
          efforts, disaster prediction, and improved public health.
