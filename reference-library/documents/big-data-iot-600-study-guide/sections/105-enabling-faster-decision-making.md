Enabling Faster Decision-Making

Visualizing data helps stakeholders make quicker, more informed decisions. Well-designed charts
and graphs can highlight key issues or opportunities, allowing teams to act faster. For example,
a sales team might use a heat map to identify high-sales regions, enabling them to focus
resources on the most promising areas.
