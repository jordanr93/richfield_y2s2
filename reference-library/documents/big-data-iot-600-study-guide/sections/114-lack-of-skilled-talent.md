Lack of Skilled Talent

Another significant barrier is the shortage of data professionals who can effectively analyze and
interpret big data. Data scientists, analysts, and engineers are essential for extracting valuable
insights from complex datasets, but there’s a high demand for these skills, and many businesses
struggle to find the right talent. Without the right expertise, companies can find it challenging to
turn big data into actionable insights that can shape business strategy.
