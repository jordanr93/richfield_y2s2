Making Complex Data Understandable

Data visualization simplifies complex information by presenting it in formats like charts, graphs,
and dashboards. For instance, instead of listing sales figures, a business might use bar or pie
charts to illustrate sales performance over a given period. This makes it easier to quickly
understand trends and outcomes without wading through raw data.
