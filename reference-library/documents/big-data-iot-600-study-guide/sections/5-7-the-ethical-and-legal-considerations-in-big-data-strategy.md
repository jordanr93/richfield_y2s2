5.7 The Ethical and Legal Considerations in Big Data Strategy

When implementing a big data strategy, organizations must carefully evaluate the ethical and
legal implications to ensure that they are not only compliant with regulations but also acting
responsibly and maintaining public trust. The collection, use, and analysis of massive amounts of
data can raise serious concerns, particularly around privacy, fairness, and transparency. Let's dive
into some of the key ethical and legal considerations.
