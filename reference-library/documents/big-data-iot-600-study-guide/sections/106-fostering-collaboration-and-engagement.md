Fostering Collaboration and Engagement

Data visualizations promote better engagement and collaboration. By using visuals, teams at all
levels can interact with the data, ask questions, and contribute ideas. Interactive dashboards, for
instance, let users explore the data themselves, which encourages deeper discussions and
collaborative problem-solving.
