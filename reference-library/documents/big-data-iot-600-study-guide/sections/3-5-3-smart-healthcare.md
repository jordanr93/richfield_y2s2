3.5.3 Smart Healthcare

IoT is transforming healthcare by enabling remote monitoring, patient tracking, and asset
management, leading to better patient care and more efficient healthcare operations.

   IoT Technologies: Wearable devices such as smartwatches track vital signs like heart rate and
   blood pressure, using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to transmit data to healthcare systems.
   Wi-Fi and cellular networks enable remote patient monitoring.

   Standards: IEEE 11073 defines communication standards for medical devices, ensuring
   compatibility between devices from different manufacturers. HL7 (Health Level Seven) is
   another standard for the exchange of clinical and administrative healthcare data.
