Data Quality and Accuracy

One of the primary challenges is ensuring that the data being used is accurate and of high quality.
Big data often comes from a variety of sources, and some of it may be incomplete, inconsistent,
or outdated. If the data is unreliable, any strategic decisions based on it can lead to poor
outcomes. Cleaning and validating large datasets can be time-consuming and resource-intensive.
