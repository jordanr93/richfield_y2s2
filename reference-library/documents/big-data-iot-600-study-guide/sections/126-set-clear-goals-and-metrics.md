Set Clear Goals and Metrics

Before diving into monitoring, make sure you have well-defined goals for what you want to
achieve with Big Data. These goals should tie directly into the overall business objectives,
whether it’s improving customer experience, optimizing operations, or driving innovation. Once
you have those goals, identify measurable metrics (KPIs) that will help you track success—things
like how quickly data is processed, the quality of your data, or the impact data has on decision-
making.
