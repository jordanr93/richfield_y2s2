Optimize Storage and Infrastructure

As data grows, so does the need for efficient storage. Regularly assess whether your storage
solutions are cost-effective and scalable. Cloud storage can be a good option to scale up and
down based on needs, but you should keep an eye on costs. Also, ensure your infrastructure is
keeping up with increasing data demands and adjust it as necessary.
