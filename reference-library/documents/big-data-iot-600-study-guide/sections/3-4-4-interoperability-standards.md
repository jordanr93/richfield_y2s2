3.4.4 Interoperability Standards

IoT systems often involve a mix of devices from different manufacturers, which is why
interoperability standards are critical. These standards allow devices and systems to work
together seamlessly.

   OneM2M: A global standard for ensuring IoT devices can communicate across various
   industries.

   ODF (Open Device Framework): Defines how devices and applications interact within an IoT
   ecosystem, ensuring compatibility.

   AllJoyn: A framework that facilitates device-to-device communication across different IoT
   ecosystems, especially in smart homes.
