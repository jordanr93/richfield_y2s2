2.5 Evaluating Security Challenges in IoT Systems
Because of its dispersed architecture, resource-constrained devices, and massive data
generation, IoT systems present serious security issues. IoT security includes safeguarding
networks, devices, and data against abuse, illegal access, and cyberattacks. The three main areas
of concern are privacy protection, authentication, and encryption.
