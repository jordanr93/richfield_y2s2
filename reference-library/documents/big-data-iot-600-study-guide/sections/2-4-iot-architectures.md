2.4 IoT Architectures

A "smart" device is a gadget that has actuators that allow for physical actions and sensors that
gather information about certain actions. This data is then sent across a network for processing.
These items cover a broad spectrum of gadgets, such as refrigerators, streetlights, structures,
automobiles, manufacturing equipment, rehabilitation tools, and other intelligent gadgets.
Although the sensors are usually built within the items, they can also be placed in the local
surroundings to keep an eye on and communicate with the things. Simple tasks like opening or
closing a door, turning on or off a light, or modifying a motor's speed could be the actions that
these systems initiate.
