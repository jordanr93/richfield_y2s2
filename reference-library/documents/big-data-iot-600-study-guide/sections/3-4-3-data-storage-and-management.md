3.4.3 Data Storage and Management

IoT devices generate vast amounts of data, and efficient storage and management are crucial for
making sense of it.

   SQL and NoSQL Databases: These are widely used to store structured and unstructured data,
   with NoSQL databases like MongoDB often preferred for their flexibility in handling large-
   scale data.

   Time Series Databases (TSDBs): Since IoT devices often generate data over time, specialized
   databases like InfluxDB or Prometheus are used to efficiently store and analyze this time-
   stamped data.
