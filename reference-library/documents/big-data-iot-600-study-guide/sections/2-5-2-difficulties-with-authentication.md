2.5.2 Difficulties with Authentication
IoT systems are only accessible by authorised people, devices, and services thanks to
authentication. Strong authentication procedures are essential for avoiding unwanted access.
Problems: Inadequate or default credentials
Users frequently forget to update the default usernames and passwords that come with many
IoT devices, leaving them open to assaults like botnets (like the Mirai botnet).
