Cultural Resistance

Some organizations may face resistance from employees who are not accustomed to working
with data or are hesitant to rely on data-driven decision-making. Employees may be used to
traditional methods, relying on intuition or past experience, and may be wary of trusting data
analytics over their own judgment. Overcoming this resistance requires fostering a data-driven
culture where decision-making is based on evidence, and staff are trained to use data effectively.
