Data Privacy and Security Concerns

Big data often includes sensitive customer information, which raises concerns about privacy and
security. As businesses gather more data, they must ensure that they comply with privacy
regulations like GDPR and protect against potential data breaches. Failing to do so not only puts
customers at risk but also damages the company’s reputation and trustworthiness.
