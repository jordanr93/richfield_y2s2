3.5.5 Smart Cities

IoT enhances urban living by improving infrastructure, transportation management, and
environmental monitoring in smart cities.

   IoT Technologies: Smart traffic lights, parking sensors, and waste management systems use
   IoT to collect and analyze data. 5G networks provide high-speed, low-latency communication
   for real-time data exchange.

   Standards: ISO 37120 provides metrics for measuring the quality of life in smart cities, while
   OneM2M enables device interoperability across city infrastructures.

Example: In a smart city, IoT-enabled traffic lights adjust based on vehicle flow, optimizing traffic
patterns. These systems communicate securely using TLS/SSL encryption to protect sensitive
data.
