Stay Flexible and Adapt to Changes

Big Data strategies aren’t set in stone. As business needs evolve, your strategy should be able to
adapt. Periodically revisit your strategy to ensure it aligns with any changes in the business. This
might involve shifting priorities or incorporating new technology. It’s also helpful to maintain
ongoing discussions with business stakeholders to ensure data is serving their needs.
