Evaluate and Adjust Analytical Models

If your strategy includes machine learning or advanced analytics, you’ll want to keep track of how
well these models are performing. Are they still delivering the insights needed? As new data
comes in, models may need adjustments to stay accurate. A/B testing can help you evaluate
different approaches and pick the one that works best.
