2.3.4 Use Models and Prototypes

For users, the interface is the most critical aspect of the system since it's where they interact
with it, often for long periods. It's essential to create models and prototypes for user
feedback. Initial designs can be presented as storyboards—sketches showing the general
layout. Feedback can be gathered through interviews, questionnaires, and direct observation.
Usability metrics, measured through software tracking user interactions, can also provide
valuable data.
