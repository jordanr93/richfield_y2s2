5.1 QUALITY ASSURANCE


In today’s competitive business world, companies are deeply focused on the quality of their
products and services. For an organization to succeed, it must ensure quality across all
aspects, including its information systems. It is essential that top management offers the
leadership, motivation, and support necessary to maintain high-quality IT resources.
