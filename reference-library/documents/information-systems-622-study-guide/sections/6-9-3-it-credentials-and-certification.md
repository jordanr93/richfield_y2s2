6.9.3 IT Credentials and Certification

In recent years, technical credentials and certification have become increasingly important for both
employers and employees in the IT field. Credentials broadly include formal degrees, diplomas, or
certificates issued by educational institutions to confirm that a certain level of education has been
achieved. Certification, on the other hand, refers to specific hardware and software skills that can be
tested and verified through exams. For example, someone might hold a two- or four-year degree in
Information Systems and also have an ISTQB Foundation Level certification, which verifies expertise
