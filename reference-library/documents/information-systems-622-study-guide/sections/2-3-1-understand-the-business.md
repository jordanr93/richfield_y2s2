2.3.1 Understand the Business

A designer must comprehend the underlying business functions and how the system supports
individual, departmental, and enterprise goals. The primary goal is to create an interface that
aids users in performing their jobs effectively. A good starting point might be analyzing a
Functional Decomposition Diagram (FDD), which visually represents business functions,
breaking them down into various levels of detail. The FDD can serve as a checklist for the tasks
that must be included in the interface design.
